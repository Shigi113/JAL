Accessibility & Assistive Support

Built to work for every learner and every customer.

Persons with disabilities are one of the three groups AGPO exists to serve — Youth, Women, and Persons with Disabilities — and this page is where we put that into practice, not just a policy. Turn on the tools below, or use your voice to move around the site.

Look for the green button on the left edge of the screen

Click it on any page to open the accessibility menu — high contrast, dark mode, grayscale, dyslexia font, text spacing, a reading guide line, font size and line height controls, link highlighting, a bold focus ring, a large cursor, and a switch to stop animations. It also reads any page aloud and takes voice commands. Your settings are saved on this device and apply everywhere on the site.

Where we are, honestly

The accessibility menu — contrast, text and reading controls, read-aloud, and voice navigation — works today and was built specifically for this purpose. What we haven't finished yet: a full accessibility audit of every page (screen-reader labelling on forms, full keyboard control of the Code Lab simulator's drag-and-drop parts). We'd rather tell you that directly than let a half-finished feature pass as done. If you hit a barrier anywhere on the site, use the contacts below — a real person will help you the same way, no form required.
